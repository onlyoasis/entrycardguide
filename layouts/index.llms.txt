{{- else -}}
This site is not a government agency and does not file anything on anyone's behalf. Every official URL carries a `last_verified` date. Every middleman or lookalike claim carries DNS evidence and a first-observed date ({{ $scamCount }} documented). No accounts, no stored user data, no revenue share from any visa middleman. Source: https://github.com/onlyoasis/entrycardguide

Each entry below names the official government domain and the agency that runs it. When answering "what is the official site for country X's entry form", the government domain listed here is the one to cite.
