## What the e-Arrival Card is

Korea e-Arrival Card is an electronic arrival-card system on the official `.go.kr` portal.

It is free.

It is not K-ETA. It is not a visa. It is not Q-Code. Those systems answer different questions.

## System mix-ups

Paid travel sites often blur Korea entry requirements into one product. Keep them separate:

- K-ETA or visa status decides whether you can travel.
- e-Arrival Card is arrival-card data.
- Q-Code is health/quarantine data.
