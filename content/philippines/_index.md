## The fast facts

- **Official URL:** `etravel.gov.ph`.
- **Cost:** free.
- **What you need:** passport, flight or vessel details, arrival or departure date, email.
- **What you get:** a QR confirmation. Screenshot it.
- **What it is not:** a visa.
