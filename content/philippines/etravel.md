## What eTravel is

eTravel is the Philippine electronic travel declaration. It collects traveler, trip, and contact information before arrival or departure.

It is free.

The official address is `etravel.gov.ph`. Do not use a `.com`, `.org`, or social media form that asks for payment to file eTravel.

## Before you touch any form

Check the address bar first. The official portal ends in `.gov.ph`.

If a page asks for a service fee, rush fee, or paid "approval" for eTravel, you are not paying the government eTravel fee. There is no eTravel fee.
