Indonesia's electronic Customs Declaration (e-CD) is a free digital form required for all arrivals — including Indonesian citizens. It replaced the paper customs form in 2022 and is filed at `ecd.beacukai.go.id`.

Indonesia also has a separate **e-Visa on Arrival (e-VOA)** at `molina.imigrasi.go.id` for nationalities that don't qualify for visa-free entry. The e-VOA has a real government fee (about $35 USD); the e-CD is free.

## The fast facts

- **Official URL:** `ecd.beacukai.go.id` — the `.go.id` suffix is restricted to Indonesian government entities.
- **Cost:** free. Always free.
- **Required for:** every arriving traveler, including Indonesian citizens. Submitted within 3 days of arrival.
- **What you need:** passport, flight number, address in Indonesia, email, about 7 minutes.
- **What you get:** a QR code emailed to you. Show it at customs.
- **e-VOA is separate:** if you need a visa on arrival ($35 USD government fee), file at `molina.imigrasi.go.id`. That's a different form, different domain, different agency. Both may be required for your trip.
