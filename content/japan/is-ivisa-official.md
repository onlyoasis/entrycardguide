## Short answer

iVisa is not Visit Japan Web. Visit Japan Web is a free Japanese government service. Visa services, eVisas, and arrival QR workflows are separate.

If a paid site says it will "handle Japan entry" for you, ask what it actually files: a visa application, a Visit Japan Web QR workflow, or just a paid checklist.

## How to check

1. The official source is under `.go.jp`.
2. Visit Japan Web is free.
3. A visa service does not replace Visit Japan Web QR setup.
4. You should control the account yourself.
