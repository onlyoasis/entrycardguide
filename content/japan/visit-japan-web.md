## What Visit Japan Web is

Visit Japan Web is the official service for Japan arrival-related QR workflows, including immigration, customs, and tax-free procedures where available.

It is free.

The stable official source is the Digital Agency page under `.go.jp`. Be careful with copied login pages or apps that ask for passport data outside the Japanese government domain.
