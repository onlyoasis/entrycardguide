## What e-Arrival is

Cambodia e-Arrival is the official digital arrival declaration. It helps collect immigration, customs, and health declaration information before you arrive.

The official address is `arrival.gov.kh`.

## Fee boundary

The arrival declaration is free. Cambodia visa fees are a separate government charge when your nationality or trip requires a visa.

If a non-`.gov.kh` page charges you just to file the arrival declaration, it is a middleman.

## What to keep

Save the e-Arrival confirmation. If you also applied for a visa, keep the visa approval separately. Do not assume one document replaces the other.
