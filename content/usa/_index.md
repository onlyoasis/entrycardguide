ESTA is the United States travel authorization for eligible Visa Waiver Program travelers. It is not a visa. Apply on the U.S. Customs and Border Protection website.

## The fast facts

- **Official total if approved:** USD $40.27. CBP charges a $4.00 processing fee, then $36.27 after approval. If the application is refused, only the $4.00 processing fee is charged.
- **Price change:** the total rose from $21.00 to $40.27 on September 30, 2025.
- **Who needs it:** eligible VWP passport holders traveling to the United States by air, sea, or land.
- **When to apply:** no later than 72 hours before departure.
- **Validity:** generally 2 years or until the passport expires, whichever comes first. ESTA for Brunei passport holders is limited to 1 year.
- **What it is not:** ESTA is not a visa and does not guarantee admission at the border.

Check whether your passport is covered by the Visa Waiver Program before applying.
