Use the CBP site and the e-passport you will carry. ESTA is a paid travel authorization, not a visa. An approved application costs USD $40.27: $4.00 to process it, then $36.27 after approval. Apply no later than 72 hours before departure.

The checker below covers the 9 field groups in `data/rules/usa.json`. It runs in your browser and sends nothing to us. The live CBP form controls the final application.

## Before payment

Check the host again: `esta.cbp.dhs.gov`. The official charge starts with the $4.00 processing fee. The $36.27 authorization fee is charged only after approval. If the result is Travel Not Authorized, only the $4.00 processing fee applies.

Save the application number after submission. Approval is usually valid for 2 years or until the passport expires, whichever comes first. Brunei passport holders receive a 1-year validity period.
